About Me

I've been programming ever since I was a teenager with the TI-84 calculators we used in high school. I am lucky that I have a profession that is my passion; it makes creating websites, programming software, and solving problems fun (I know what you're thinking...this guy codes for fun?!).

Throughout my life, I've worked in graphic design and teaching, two things that furthered my devotion in creating high-quality products and helping others. I now bring this drive to web design and software development. I will work meticulously to ensure the code that I write is clean and efficient, because that is what I enjoy doing!

If I'm not at the computer typing away, you'll find me playing board games, singing, and spending time with my loved ones.
